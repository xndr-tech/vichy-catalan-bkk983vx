About Vichy Catalan

A Legacy of Purity and Taste

Vichy Catalan originates from the thermal springs of Caldes de Malavella, Girona, Spain, where it has been flowing naturally for centuries. Discovered and bottled by Dr. Modest Furest i Roca in 1881, this water quickly gained recognition for its exceptional mineral composition and delightful, naturally carbonated effervescence.

With over 140 years of history, Vichy Catalan has become a symbol of quality and Spanish heritage. Its unique balanced flavor and healthful properties make it a favorite among chefs, sommeliers, and health-conscious individuals worldwide.

Experience the tradition and natural goodness that define every drop of Vichy Catalan, a true gift from the earth.

The Natural Benefits of Vichy Catalan

Beyond its refreshing taste, Vichy Catalan is celebrated for its unique mineral composition, offering a range of health advantages that set it apart.

Digestive Aid

Naturally rich in bicarbonate, Vichy Catalan helps neutralize stomach acidity, aiding digestion and reducing discomfort after meals.

High Mineral Content

Packed with essential minerals like sodium, potassium, calcium, and magnesium, vital for maintaining body balance and wellness.

Electrolyte Replenishment

Its unique mineral profile makes it an excellent choice for replenishing electrolytes, especially after physical activity.

Alkalizing Properties

Helps maintain the body's pH balance, contributing to overall health and vitality.

Naturally Sparkling

Unlike many sparkling waters, Vichy Catalan is naturally carbonated at its source, providing a distinct and pleasant effervescence.

Culinary Favorite

Its fine bubbles and unique taste enhance culinary experiences, pairing perfectly with gourmet meals.
